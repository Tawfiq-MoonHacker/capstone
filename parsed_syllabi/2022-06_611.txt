Course Overview and Prerequisites This course will cover advanced techniques in representing and indexing data in JSON and full-text fields.  We will also review and cover the performance of database operations across all types of SQL queries.  We also compare and contrast relational and non-relational approaches to database and discuss when to use various different database technologies.
